Introduction

AuricDefence is an innovative platform designed to revolutionize how cybersecurity incidents are reported and managed. Unlike traditional systems, AuricDefence uses blockchain technology to ensure that every report is secure, anonymous, and tamper-proof. Our goal is to build trust, improve transparency, and enable rapid response to cybersecurity threats for individuals and organizations worldwide.
